Biryani

Chicken

Wash the chicken and marinate a day before cooking, you should chop the pieces and allow the garlic and ginger paste along with chillipowder,salt into it. Add little curd and some masala and store it in fridge with an lid.

Lamb

Same process as chicken but you should add extra spices and chillipowder to it and curd is the main thing that gives the falvour to the biryani. Before cooking you should boil the rice, use basmathi rice for tasty.

Prawns

The process is different and the prawns will cook in minimum of thirty minutes, before two hours marinate the prawns with spices according to the taste and avoid curd. Hint, you should wash with turmaric powder and with lemon.
